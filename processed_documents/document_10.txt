Section 19. Judge
“Judge”.–The word “Judge” denotes not only every person who is officially 
designated as a Judge, but also every person who is empowered by law to give,
in any legal proceeding, civil or criminal, a definitive judgment, or a judgment 
which, if not appealed against, would be definitive, or a judgment which, if 
confirmed by some other authority, would be definitive, or
who is one of a body of persons, which body of persons is empowered by law 
to give such a judgment.
Illustrations
(a) A Collector exercising jurisdiction in a suit under Act 10 of 1859, is a judge.
