Section 55A. Definition of appropriate Government
155A. Definition of “appropriate Government”.- In sections fifty-four and fifty-
five the expression “appropriate Government” means, –
(a) In case where the sentence is a sentence of death or is for an offence 
against any law relating to a matter to which the executive power of the Union 
extends, the Central Government; and
(b) In case where the sentence (whether of death or not) is for an offence 
against any law relating to a matter to which the executive power of the State 
extends, the Government of the State within which the offender is sentenced.
