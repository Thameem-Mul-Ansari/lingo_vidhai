Illustration
A writes his name on the back of a bill of exchange payable to his order. The 
meaning of the endorsement, as explained by mercantile usage, is that the bill 
is to be paid to the holder. The endorsement is a document, and must be 
construed in the same manner as if the words “pay to the holder” or words to 
that effect had been written over the signature.
Section 29A. Electronic record
129A. Electronic record.- The words “electronic record” shall have the meaning
assigned to them in clause (t) of sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Information
Technology Act, 2000.
