In every case in which an offender is punishable with imprisonment which 
may be of either description, it shall be competent to the Court which 
sentences such offender to direct in the sentence that such imprisonment shall
be wholly rigorous, or that such imprisonment shall be wholly simple, or that 
any part of such imprisonment shall be rigorous and the rest simple

Section 63. Amount of fine
Where no sum is expressed to which a fine may extend, the amount of fine to 
which the offender is liable is unlimited, but shall not be excessive.
