Section 3. Punishment of offences committed beyond, but which by law may be tried 
within, India
Any person liable, by any  1(Indian law) to be tried for an offence committed 
beyond 2(India) shall be dealt with according to the provisions of this Code for 
any act committed beyond  2(India) in the same manner as if such act had been 
committed within  3[India].
